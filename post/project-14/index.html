Project XIV: Research Project - St. Christopher's Hospital for Children Process Mapping Project

December 6, 2023

Date: April - June 2023

Right after I finished my master’s program, I worked as a research assistant with Dr. Hande Benson and Drexel Solutions Institute. We worked on a data analytics project aiming to help St. Christopher Hospital of Children in Philadelphia assess patient flow, examine opportunities for greater productivity and effectiveness to decrease LWOT (patients that leave without treatment), evaluate strategic opportunities and tools for staff communications about patients’ progress within the hospital system and streamline interrelated processes. My role was to help Dr. Benson with data management and analytics, including cleaning the data, performing exploratory data analysis, creating data visualization, and building machine learning models to identify the key features influencing the LWOTs.
